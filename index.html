What is Vaporwave?

Vaporwave is a microgenre of electronic music, a visual art style, and an Internet meme that emerged in the early 2010s. It is defined by its eclectic mix of retro aesthetics, early Internet imagery, and a nostalgic fascination with the late 80s and early 90s pop culture.
